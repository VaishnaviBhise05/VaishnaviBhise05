# 💫 About Me:
I am Vaishnavi Bhise, a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) student in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Sanjivani University, India. I am passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, IoT, Cloud Computing, and Software Development. I enjoy solving real-world problems by building innovative, technology-driven solutions with a strong focus on sustainability, education, and smart infrastructure.<br><br>I have developed several end-to-end projects, including an AI-powered Teaching Assistant (Sahayak) for personalized education in multi-grade classrooms, a Solar-Powered EV Charging Station with an integrated mobile application, a Smart Microgrid Energy Monitoring System, a Library Management System, a QR Code Generator, and other AI- and IoT-based applications. These projects have strengthened my skills in full-stack development, Firebase, Python, JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, databases, and AI technologies.<br><br>I actively participate in hackathons, technical competitions, research initiatives, and innovation challenges. My work has been recognized with first prize at an industrial hackathon, motivating me to continue creating impactful solutions. I am also interested in publishing research papers and contributing to emerging technologies that address real-world challenges.<br><br>I am a quick learner who enjoys exploring new technologies, collaborating with teams, and continuously improving my technical and problem-solving abilities. My goal is to build intelligent, scalable, and user-centric solutions while growing as an AI engineer and technology innovator.<br>
